Meet Us!

Lori and John, a delightful and endearing couple who have been married for over 40 years and weathered the sands of time together, embarked on a dream-come-true adventure when they decided to open their own bed and breakfast. After traveling the world together, they wanted to settle on providing others with unique experiences and smiles. With a shared passion for creating unforgettable experiences and a love for their community, Lori and John transformed a historic residence into a haven for travelers seeking respite and connection.
